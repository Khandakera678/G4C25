Looking for a fun and simpe game to play? Than Roll the Snowball is a perfect choice , a cute and simpe game to relax with.

THEME

The theme of the game is paceformers. It is shown by the corrupted mole trying to detsroy everything in its path but the snowball through working hard and collecting points, going through mazes defeats the corrupted mole eater creating peace atlast to his homeland.

MY WHY

I created this game with idea of a creating a fun and simple game to play. Later as I kept adding on to it I decided to give it a lore and background. This game was created to show how corruption can be bad. Shown by the corrupted mole who wants to eat Snowball even after eating and destroying his whole family.
